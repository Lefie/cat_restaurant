Cat Restaurant

Bonjour! Welcome to the cat Restaurant!

This project is meant to be a French Vocabulary learning game that introduces you french food items.

If you are a beginner at French, please click "Learn" to check out a list of food item and their french translation

Make sure you have mastered them, especially their correct spelling because it will be useful during the game

When you are comfortable with all the words and their spellings, go ahead and click play!

Your goal is to take the cat's orders, but first, you need to determine whether or not the order items are spelled correctly

If they are spelled correctly, press the green circle and serve them the corresponding food items

Otherwise, reject their orders!

If you by accident accept the wrong order or reject the correct orders, you will lose coins

Have fun learning!
